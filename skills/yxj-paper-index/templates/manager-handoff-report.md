## Required vocabulary

Use these words precisely and do not collapse them:

- `validated`
- `ingested`
- `recommended`
- `blocked`
- `owner-gated`
- `external-gated`

Split next steps into:

- `auto-continuable`
- `requires paper-owner decision`
- `hard-gated/prohibited until explicit authorization`
