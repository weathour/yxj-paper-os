# yxj-paper-interview

Ask only blocking paper-owner questions. Record answers into the decision ledger; record safe assumptions separately.

## Ask for explicit confirmation when deciding

- final motivation or contribution spine;
- target venue commitment;
- claim scope beyond available evidence;
- private/raw source copying policy;
- active install, uninstall, publish, external service, destructive action, or Team launch gates.

## Output

Produce a concise decision record with `decision_id`, `question`, `answer`, `scope`, `affected_artifacts`, and `remaining_blockers`.
